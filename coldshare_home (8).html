ColdShare is a hyperlocal marketplace that helps cold storage owners monetize idle chamber capacity while enabling farmers, traders, processors, and exporters to easily find and book short-term temperature-controlled storage. Starting with the Nashik grape cluster, our platform connects nearby businesses with available storage based on location, temperature requirements, and booking duration, reducing post-harvest losses and improving cold storage utilization across the region.
